FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about AI Image Studio.

Is AI Image Studio free to use?

Yes! We offer a generous free tier that allows you to process images daily. For heavy usage and advanced features, check out our premium plans.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. Your images are processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. We prioritize your privacy and data security above all.

What image formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, WebP, and most common image formats. Output can be downloaded in PNG or JPEG format.

How do I get technical support?

You can reach our support team via the contact form above, email us at support@aiimagestudio.com, or use our live chat feature (available for premium users).
